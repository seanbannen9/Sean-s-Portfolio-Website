Knight's Brew Coffee

Winter 2024

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

This project was completed in partnership with Amy Ayala-Gallardo as a part of the pixal pals program

Challenge: create branding for either a coffee or boba shop of our own design, with a style guide(s), and mock ups of merchandise

Process: At the start of our project me and my partner and mentor assigned for the project Amy started sketching out a vairety of ideas, we started by sketching concepts for both a boba and coffee shop, before deciding to further develop our concepts for coffee. (Below are the sketches I personally made)

Once I had a few concepts for coffee shop logos I liked I made a few concept logos in Adobe Illustrator, out the logos I worked on I was most invested in the development of the logo with a knight helmet, which was inspired by my roomate showing me coffee art during my sketching process. I even had a name in mind already called knights brew

Once time came to choose a logo, me and my partner decided to move forward with the Kinghts Brew concept becuase it was the one we both enjoyed the most. We then decided that I will work on the style guide, featuring two alternate color schemes, and the she would make the merchandise mock ups using templates for photoshop.
