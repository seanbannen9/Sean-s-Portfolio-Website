Duel of the Nerve Men

Spring 2023

Wire and Wood

Challenge: Create a functional mechanical toy inspired by the works of Alexander Calder using only wire and wood.

Process: As this was the first time I was working with wire, I had no idea how to bring my idea of a Rock 'em Sock 'em robots style dueling game to life. This resulted in me consulting with my teacher a lot, which led me to researching an adjacent type of physical art called wirligigs. After watching a ton of videos to figure out how they work and how they can be made, I was able to figure out the concept for how the toy would work.

Figuring out how to make the concept work was difficult. Eventually, after taking the advice of my professor, I looked into wooden mechanisms called whirligigs that gave me inspiration for the function of my toy. Figuring out how to translate that into wire was difficult, and as I worked on making the figures and mechanisms for the project, I had to engage in physics knowledge and consult with my professor in order to get the mechanism right.

Once the mechanism was properly constructed and bolted onto the board. I added some finished visual touches and the project was complete with the action function working properly!
